VISION is a team comprised of six members with unique and significant talents to perform our many missions. With our skills, we are able to solve any problems and break through corruption and fraud.
We are Lilith, Quinn, Eve, Lana, Mavis and Nakia.
We are VISION.

Lilith

Lilith is 16, and is an expert investigator. She has an incredible mind and can solve mysteries in the blink of an eye.

Quinn

Quinn is 16 years old. She is the head of the VISION team, and her role includes selecting targets, planning missions, and carrying out and leading those plans.

Eve

Eve is 14 years old, and the team’s manager. She helps in mission-planning, as well as budgeting funds, delegating tasks, and organizing briefings and meetings.

Lana

Lana is 15 years of age and an incredibly talented spy. She can morph into any person and blend into any surrounding as quickly as lightning.

Mavis

Mavis is 14 years old, and she is the resident computer expert. She can hack her way into just about anything, and is a fantastic programmer.

Nakia

Nakia is 16 years old and the team’s combat expert. She ensures the team is able to defend and protect themselves and the organization, should the need arise.
